Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git is very useful when you want to see how a file changes overtime and also it acts as a place to save files in case local files get corrupted.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to basically every field. One example is the medical field. They need to create tables of data to keep track of what symptoms a certain illness creates and be able to compare it to a patient's symptoms.
